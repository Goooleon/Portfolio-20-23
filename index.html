myTomorrows Search

In collaboration with myTomorrows, the Master Thesis explores interactive approaches to engage Healthcare professionals in providing Explicit Relevance Feedback on search results, which can be fed to the system for closing the feedback loop of system optimization with human involvement. Thesis title: Improving Search Relevance Feedback through Human Centered Design.

Brux No More

Brux No more is a medical wearable device aimed at monitoring nighttime bruxism and hindering the negative behavior by transmitting gentle stimulus to help sufferers with night time teeth grinding get rid of it and live in a healthier way.

#Medical device     #Bruxism     #Healthcare

Miáo

Miáo is a ceramic dining set design inspired by the ethnic Miao culture, their lifestyle, tales and daily objects. We selected and abstracted elements representing their culture and had them merged in the design of Miáo. The final objects were hand crafted in Jingdezhen with the help of craftsman there.

the Stool

the Stool is produced in around 6 hours from the very beginning idea to sketches to the final object that could bear an adult’s weight.
